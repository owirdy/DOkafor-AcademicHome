I am a first-year PhD candidate at the **Stanford Computational Genomics Group**, where my research focuses on **computational biology and genomics**. I graduated from the **University of Cape Town (UCT)** in December 2024.

My research interests include:
- Single-Cell RNA Sequencing Analysis
- Deep Learning for Protein Structure
- Population Genetics

## Education

- **Ph.D. in Biomedical Informatics**, Stanford University (2025-Present)
- **B.Sc.**, University of Cape Town (2020-2024)
